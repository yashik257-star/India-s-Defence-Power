HAL Tejas (Light Combat Aircraft)

Tejas is a light multirole combat aircraft developed in India by the Aeronautical Development Agency and produced by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). It serves as a modern indigenous fighter for the IAF.

HAL Dhruv (Advanced Light Helicopter)

Dhruv is an Indian-designed utility helicopter produced by HAL. It has multiple military and civil variants and is used for transport, search-and-rescue, and other roles.

HAL HTT-40 (Basic Trainer)

The HTT-40 is a turboprop basic trainer aircraft developed by HAL to train future pilots — intended to replace older trainer types.

For technical specs, production numbers and latest developments, consult official HAL/DRDO/IAF sources.
