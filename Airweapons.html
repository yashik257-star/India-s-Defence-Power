DRDO Astra (Air-to-Air Missile)

Astra is an Indian beyond-visual-range, air-to-air missile developed by DRDO and in service with the IAF. It is designed to counter modern aerial threats and can be launched from multiple fighter platforms.

AKASH (Surface-to-Air Missile)

Akash is a medium-range surface-to-air missile system developed in India to provide air defence against aircraft and cruise missiles. It has been developed and produced domestically.

Pinaka (Rocket System)

Pinaka is a multi-barrel rocket launcher system developed in India for battlefield support and area saturation; it is used by Indian armed forces and has evolved with Indian development efforts.

This page highlights a few well-known systems; India has an ongoing defence R&D and production ecosystem with many additional projects and variants.
