Indian Army Indigenous Arsenal

Showcasing indigenously built weapons & technologies strengthening India.

The Indian Army, the world's largest all-volunteer land force, is India's primary land-based military, responsible for national security, border defense, and disaster relief, operating under the President as Commander-in-Chief.

Known for bravery, discipline, and operating in extreme terrains, it has a rich history stemming from the British Indian Army and plays crucial roles in UN peacekeeping and humanitarian aid, embodying the motto "Service Before Self".
