History of Indian Navy

  1. Ancient Maritime Tradition

    2. India traded with Mesopotamia during the Indus Valley Civilization. Cholas had powerful navies.

  2. Navy During British Rule

    3. The Royal Indian Navy (RIN) protected British trade routes and colonial interests.

  3. Role in World Wars

    4. RIN supported Britain in WW-I and WW-II and expanded rapidly.

  4. Post-Independence (1947)

    5. RIN was divided between India and Pakistan. Renamed Indian Navy in 1950.

  5. 1971 War – Major Victory

    6. Operation Trident & Python destroyed Pakistan Navy. Navy Day is celebrated on 4 December.

  6. Modernization

    7. India builds its own destroyers, submarines & aircraft carriers.

  7. Indian Navy Today

    8. A powerful blue-water navy leading in humanitarian missions and maritime security.
